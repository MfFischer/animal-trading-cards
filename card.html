Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are known for their friendly disposition and intelligence, but did you know they were originally bred in Scotland during the 19th century for hunting? Their keen retrieving skills were valued by hunters for fetching game birds from both water and land.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 55–75 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 10–12 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated

Golden Retrievers are well-mannered, loyal companions that are excellent with children and other dogs. They require lots of exercise and love to play.
